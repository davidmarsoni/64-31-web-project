Slime Attack

By Marsoni David and Fernández Rodríguez Zanya

The game project we're developing takes it's inspiration from iconic pixelated platform games such as Castlevania and Mario Bros.

The objective of the game is quite straightforward: One must successfully navigate through various levels, defeat enemies, and avoid obstacles, all while striving to stay alive.

The gameplay incorporates some basic features, such as:

  • Character Control: The player can move their character around the game’s world, jumping over obstacles and using abilities to overcome challenges and defeat enemies.
  • Level Progression: Players progress through a series of levels, each with its unique layout, challenges, and enemies.
  • Enemies: They will spawn and try to kill you
  • Health System: The character has a health bar that depletes when encountering enemies or obstacles. The game ends when the health bar is empty, and the player has finished all the levels

These features meld together to create an immersive, engaging, and entertaining gameplay experience that keeps players engrossed and coming back for more.

The pixel art design of the game pays homage to platformers, it's retro style successfully bringing back the nostalgia associated with these classical games.

Regardless of whether you are an old fan of these classic adventures, or a new player yearning for a hint of past times, our game promises an unmatched experience full of excitement and challenge.

Interested in our progress? You can follow along and check out the project's source code on our Github webpage.
